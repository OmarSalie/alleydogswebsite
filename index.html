Who are we?

More than just a competitive esports clan, Alleydog's Gaming is a community of South African gamers and esports fans. What began as a group of friends competing online evolved into a leading esports clan with the plan to become one of the biggest esports organizations in South Africa. With successful teams in Overwatch, Valorant, Rocket League, League of Legends, Rainbow 6 Siege and Call of Duty, ADG esports is shaping up to be the place to be for the best gamers in South Africa.
